It's wise to take a guide... You wouldn't plan to climb a high and challenging mountain without taking a skilled guide with you. It's the same with the Bible. Everyone needs a little help, especially when we're talking about understanding difficult passages and applying them to contemporary living. Here is some of the best writing in Encounter with God over the previous years - providing a stimulating, in-depth approach to Bible reading. Enjoy the exhilaration of gaining new understanding of God's eternal purposes. This book uses exposition from some of today’s best Bible teachers, including Michael Baughen, Steve Motyer and Jo Bailey Wells. It is also a great introduction to all the main literary genres in the Bible: law, history, psalms, prophecy, apocalyptic, gospel and epistle as it covers key biblical books in a fantastic overview of God’s wonderful plan of salvation. Contains 365 undated readings from previous years' editions of Encounter with God notes, plus a helpful introduction.
