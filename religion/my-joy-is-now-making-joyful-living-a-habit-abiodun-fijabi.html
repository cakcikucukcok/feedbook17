What has happened to our joy? Did we lose it to mountains of pains and sorrows we experience? Or, to wrong attitudes? May be we are looking for it in wrong places. May be we are not even sure of what we want. The confusions about joy have only compounded our search for happiness-the type that endures the seasons. We seem to have become content with a joyless life with occasional bursts of excitement. Can we prolong our happiness through thick and thin? Can a joyful living become a habit? Can our joy be now? Not tomorrow? Not the next moment? In MY Joy Is Now, Abiodun Fijabi, gives us a hope, In a simple and lucid way, he challenges us to a new attitude and a new walk with God that make joy not an occasional but a constant reality.
