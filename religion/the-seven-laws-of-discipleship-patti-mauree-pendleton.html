The Seven Laws of Discipleship: The Ultimate Guide for Spiritual Transformation gives voice to a collection of truths that shape the lives of disciples. The author, Patti Mauree Pendleton, following the lead of the Holy Spirit, has paired her own personal reflections with the seven laws of discipleship.

These laws reveal that ones life reflects Christ, ones life is not ones own, God requires faith, the Christian life calls for forgiveness, Christian discipleship entails preparation, growing in ones relationship with Christ leads to discovering the secrets of the kingdom, and disciples surrender to the love of God.

Towards the end of The Seven Laws of Discipleship the author shares a brief thought: Reminder: it is said, new habits take 30 days to root--give yourself 30 days to get the good habit ingrained into your life!! Her advice, reflecting her own experience that gave rise to the books reflections, offers a way to make this book serve as an ultimate guide. In truth, reading The Seven Laws of Discipleship means taking action. Pick up your Bible, find a concordance, immerse yourself in the laws of discipleship, and then you will discover the path that leads you to spiritual transformation.
