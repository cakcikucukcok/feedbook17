The story rolls on from the deserts of Australia from an early career supplying the meat trade. This part will appeal to rural Australia to starting a small business in the city to the best state to live in and gold, prospecting also my experiences with Australia's allusive black panthers, which I have given my opinions and some evidence while I lived amongst them.
