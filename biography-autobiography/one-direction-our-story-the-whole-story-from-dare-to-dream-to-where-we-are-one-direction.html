From teen hopefuls auditioning for The X Factor to award-winning international sensations whose names are on everyone’s lips, no one can dispute that Louis, Liam, Harry, Zayn and Niall have had a phenomenal journey.

In Dare to Dream, Harry, Niall, Zayn, Liam and Louis each open up about who they are, where they come from, and the X Factor experience, telling the 1D story up until they became the UK’s biggest boyband.
