It's never a good idea to be overly–reliant on technology while traveling! Look-up words quickly and easily with this Korean travel dictionary.

Tuttle's Mini Korean Dictionary is ideal for any application where a handy and portable dictionary is required. Intended for use by tourists, students, and business people traveling to Korea or as a Korean language study reference Mini Korean Dictionary is an essential tool for communicating in Korean and a great way to learn Korean. It's useful pocket-sized format, and easy-to-read type will make translating Korean much easier.

In addition to being an excellent English to Korean dictionary, and Korean to English dictionary Mini Korean Dictionary contains essential notes on the Korean language, Korean grammar, and Korean pronunciation. All Korean words are written in a Romanized form as well as Korean script (hangul) so that in the case of difficulties the book can directly be shown to the person the user is trying to communicate with.

This mini dictionary contains the following essential features:

  • Bidirectional Korean to English and English to Korean
  • Over 12,000 crucial Korean words, as well as useful Korean expressions and idioms
  • A basic overview of Korean grammar and pronunciation
  • All the latest Korean social media and computer terms
  • May be used for all U.S. ESL standardized testing
