Fresh off a breakup, Sienna James doesn't expect to find the star of all of her adolescent fantasies at the exotic resort where she's holidaying. And she certainly doesn't expect her ex to show up to convince her it's not over. Faced with two hot men offering to fulfil her every fantasy, what's a woman supposed to do?

Josh Lye never forgot Sienna. He wants her just as much today as he did all those years ago. They're adults now, and there's nothing to stop them getting together--except her ex, who has the wildest proposition.

When Ben Cowley arrives at Bandicoot Cove, he's determined to win Sienna back. He knows she still loves him, but he also sees the way she looks at Josh.

Sleeping with both men at the same time in order to decide between them could be a recipe for disaster. Or it could be the start of something unexpected.

Each book in the Bandicoot Cove series is a standalone story that can be enjoyed out of order.
