If you are a romantic at heart and you like the idea of finding a soul mate or if you are someone who yearns to find that special someone who can spoil you with riches then you are going to love Where to Find a Rich Man in New York City. In this book you will learn to think like the rich New Yorker thinks and be in places where that Rich New Yorker is going to see you and meet you. Can I guarantee that you will catch your Rich Man no that's up to you. But by reading this book I can guarantee that you will have a better understanding of what you need to do to make yourself desirable to a Rich New Yorker and where you have to be to find a Rich New Yorker. Stop day dreaming about getting what you want and go after it.
