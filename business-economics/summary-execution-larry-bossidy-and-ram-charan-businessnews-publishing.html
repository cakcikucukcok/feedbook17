Complete summary of Larry Bossidy and Ram Chaean's book: " Execution: The Discipline of Getting Things Done"

The review of the ideas in  Larry Bossidy and Ram Chaean's book: " Execution" shows that execution - or more frequently, the lack of execution- is  the biggest issue facing businesses today. In fact it is the gap between what a company promises and the results it actually achieves. This summary highlights that, to be able to execute consistently well, a business needs to have in place three key building blocks: leaders who are hands-on and who understand execution, a corporate culture which values execution highly and the right people in the right places. In all, it shows that unless the right people focus on the right details at the right time, execution just won’t happen. 

Added-value of this summary: • Save time • Understand key concepts • Expand your business knowledge 

To learn more, read "Excecution" and expound a corporate vision which is grounded squarely in reality rather than wishes.
