When their family forgets to turn on the invisible fence one beautiful fall day, golden retrievers Riley and Ginger run away from home and have a big adventure!

Riley races down the road with his mother, Ginger, close behind. When they arrive at their favorite playground, Lake Park, they play all dayuntil it gets dark. Suddenly, Riley and Ginger have to experience the challenges of running away from home. They will be afraid of the dark scary night, face all kinds of bad weather, and learn the difference between a cat and a skunk.

In this enchanting childrens picture book based on a true story and illustrated with a colorful art quilt, two golden retrievers embark on a rollicking three-day adventure neither will soon forget.
