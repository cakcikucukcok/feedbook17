On this 31st day of August in the 150th year of Wilbras the 1st’s reign, a magician by the name of Exir was calmly remembering how his country used to be, a powerful and prosperous country called Dorado, when he spotted a gigantic and particularly dark cloud lingering around Princess Launa’s castle. Had the Invaders returned ? Was the protective dome compromised ? His blood flowed ice cold. THIS IS HOW THE ADVENTURE BEGINS for Andrick, when his father reveals the existence of a secret dragon, a live specimen of those creatures they believed were invented to liven up conversations. Suddenly driven by curiosity, Andrick decides to set out on his own in search of Infera, the dragon of the lost legend. His twin sister Nina, himself and the entire community of enchanters will face many obstacles as well as completely unexpected, heart-wrenching events. Don’t miss out the next Dragon’s sequel !
