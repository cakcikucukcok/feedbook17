Slap dab. What is it? Well, it can be anything you want it to be! It can be something youve found, something youve lost, something funny, something strange and something messy and muddy especially if left out in the rain! Get ready to play in the slap dab and meet such characters as Cock-a-Doodle-Dog, Boe Boe and Hissy with the story of the rattle snake who had lost his rattle, Lenny the Lying Lion, a Yorkshire Alligator in New York, Nit Wit and Flea Bag, Maw Maw the lazy magpie, Boney the dinosaur with a weak bladder, White Rabbit White Rabbit White Rabbit and the Crisscross Kiss Monsters, as well as a rhyme here and there. Of course you may meet anyone of them when you walk down the street today, but just in case you dont, why not read on and meet some new friends who may surprise you!
