Intimidating Obscurity a Pursue to Eternity, revolves around an independent woman, Stella Stewart, who hardly steps into her college life when she is informed of her mothers mysterious death. After a few days two more deaths of similar patterns take place, unfortunately she loses all her dear ones one by one. All her moaning vanishes in a glimpse when Rey enters her life and she falls for him. Her life takes a big twist when she dejects him due to a mysterious phone call. After a few days she gets married to Robert Patrick who swears to make her life worse than hell along with the company of Kimaya, who loved Rey too and joins him to separate Rey and Stella. Heartbroken Rey marries Kimaya. After a year Stella is found dead. Rey almost forgets his past love when he discovers Stellas diary one day and which opens her entire story before him. Will Rey succeed in rescuing Stella from Robert before its too late?
