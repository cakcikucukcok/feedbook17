This book is a practical manual, not a philosophical book based upon theories. This book is meant for both men and women whose most pressing need is to be that good parent and be able to bring up successful individuals from the children they have been blessed with. It is for those who need to spring into the action of being good parents without going through the painful process by which these conclusions were reached. It is expected that the reader will take the fundamental statements upon faith, just as they would take the lessons learnt from life as a reality. So please ensure that you enter parenthood with a set of expectations. This book is not meant to scare you, but to educate you and help you grow, use it to top up what you know.
