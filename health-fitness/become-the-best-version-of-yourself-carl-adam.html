Being the best version of yourself is not an objective that we can reach overnight. As a human being, we first have to accept that we aren’t perfect, and perfection will never be our truth. Being the best version of yourself is not about perfection but more about finding your true self.

The true self can only be found when we choose to remove the mask that we’ve been wearing for a long time. That mask comes with behaviors and wounds that only time and hard work can heal. Don’t be afraid to face your shadows and bring to light the aspect of yourself that needs to heal.
